About the event

An unforgettable evening with the maestro of melody, Arijit Singh, brings his soothing and powerful voice to the stage. Known for his versatility and ability to connect with listeners on a deep emotional level, Arijit Singh will perform his biggest hits from Bollywood soundtracks, including romantic ballads, heart-touching melodies, and soul-stirring tunes.

Expect an intimate and electrifying atmosphere as Arijit delivers his classics, such as Tum Hi Ho, Channa Mereya, Raabta, Phir Le Aya Dil, and many more. With a blend of acoustic and grand orchestral arrangements, the concert will feature a full live band, creating a mesmerizing experience for music lovers
